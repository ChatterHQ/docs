---
title: "Loyalty"
description: "Run a merchant's loyalty program — configuration, tiers, points, rewards, and redemptions."
---

The loyalty domain lets you manage a merchant's rewards program end to end: how points are earned, the tiers customers progress through, the rewards they can redeem, and the fulfillment of those redemptions.

## Configuration

- `GET /v1/loyalty/manage/config` — get (or lazily create) the merchant's loyalty configuration.
- `PATCH /v1/loyalty/manage/config` — update how the program behaves, including points-per-action rules.

## Tiers

Tiers are the levels customers move through (for example, Silver → Gold).

- `GET /v1/loyalty/manage/tiers` — list tiers.
- `POST /v1/loyalty/manage/tiers` — create a tier.
- `PATCH /v1/loyalty/manage/tiers/{tierId}` — update a tier.
- `DELETE /v1/loyalty/manage/tiers/{tierId}` — delete a tier.

## Points

- `POST /v1/loyalty/manage/points/award` — manually award points to a customer.
- `POST /v1/loyalty/manage/points/deduct` — deduct points from a customer.
- `GET /v1/loyalty/manage/customers/{customerId}/loyalty` — view a customer's loyalty detail (balance, tier, history).

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.usechatter.app/v1/loyalty/manage/points/award \
  -H "X-Api-Key: $CHATTER_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{ "customerId": "cust_...", "points": 500, "reason": "Promo" }'
```

## Rewards and redemptions

Rewards make up the catalog customers spend points on; redemptions are the requests to claim them.

- `GET /v1/loyalty/manage/rewards` · `POST /v1/loyalty/manage/rewards` — list and create rewards.
- `GET`/`PATCH`/`DELETE /v1/loyalty/manage/rewards/{rewardId}` — manage a reward.
- `GET /v1/loyalty/manage/redemptions` — list redemption requests.
- `POST /v1/loyalty/manage/redemptions/{redemptionId}/fulfill` — mark a redemption fulfilled.
- `POST /v1/loyalty/manage/redemptions/{redemptionId}/cancel` — cancel a redemption and refund the points.

<Tip>
  A typical integration reads `config`, seeds `tiers` and `rewards`, then awards
  points on your own business events and fulfills redemptions as they come in.
</Tip>
