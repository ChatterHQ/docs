---
title: "Marketplace"
description: "Manage stores, product and service listings, and digital assets."
---

The marketplace domain is where a merchant's catalog lives. It has three parts: **stores** (storefronts), **unified listings** (the products and services in a store), and **digital assets** (downloadable goods attached to listings).

## Stores

A store is a single storefront belonging to the merchant. Most other marketplace calls are scoped to a store via a `storeId`.

- `GET /v1/marketplace/stores` — list stores (paginated, filterable).
- `POST /v1/marketplace/stores` — create a store.
- `POST /v1/marketplace/stores/check-slug-availability` — check whether a store slug (its URL handle) is free before creating.
- `GET /v1/marketplace/stores/{id}` — fetch one store.
- `PUT /v1/marketplace/stores/{id}` — update a store.

```bash
curl "https://api.usechatter.app/v1/marketplace/stores?page=1&limit=25" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $CHATTER_API_KEY"
```

## Unified listings

A **unified listing** represents a product or service offered in a store, including its media, pricing, and customization options.

- `POST /v1/marketplace/unified` — create a listing.
- `GET /v1/marketplace/unified/my-listings` — list the merchant's listings.
- `GET /v1/marketplace/unified/{id}` — fetch one listing.
- `PUT /v1/marketplace/unified/{id}` — update a listing.
- `DELETE /v1/marketplace/unified/{id}` — remove a listing.

## Digital assets

Digital assets are downloadable files (for example, a PDF or license) attached to a listing.

- `POST /v1/marketplace/digital-assets/upload` — upload a digital asset.

## Typical flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or select a store">
    Check the slug, then `POST /v1/marketplace/stores`. Note the store's `id`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add listings">
    `POST /v1/marketplace/unified` for each product or service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach digital goods (optional)">
    Upload files with `POST /v1/marketplace/digital-assets/upload`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  See the exact request bodies, filters, and response schemas for each of these
  operations in the **API Reference** tab under *Marketplace*.
</Tip>
