---
title: "Orders"
description: "Retrieve orders and drive vendor fulfillment — confirm, update, track, cancel, and settle suborders."
---

When a customer buys from a merchant, the order is split into **suborders** — one per vendor/store fulfilling part of it. The Partner API's order endpoints are vendor-facing: you work with the suborders your merchant is responsible for.

## Reading orders

- `GET /v1/orders/vendor/suborders` — list the merchant's suborders (paginated, filterable by `status` and date range).
- `GET /v1/orders/suborders/{subOrderId}` — fetch one suborder.
- `GET /v1/orders/vendor/stats` — order statistics for the vendor.

```bash
curl "https://api.usechatter.app/v1/orders/vendor/suborders?status=pending&page=1" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $CHATTER_API_KEY"
```

## Fulfillment lifecycle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    `POST /v1/orders/suborders/{subOrderId}/confirm` — accept the suborder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update status">
    `PATCH /v1/orders/suborders/{subOrderId}/status` — move it through your
    fulfillment states.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add tracking">
    `POST /v1/orders/suborders/{subOrderId}/tracking` — attach carrier and
    tracking details once shipped.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Cancellations and settlement

- `PATCH /v1/orders/suborders/{subOrderId}/cancel` — cancel a suborder with a refund.
- `POST /v1/orders/suborders/{subOrderId}/retry-settlement` — retry settlement for a delivered suborder if the initial payout didn't complete.

<Note>
  Status transitions are validated server-side — for example, you can't add
  tracking to a suborder that hasn't been confirmed. Check the **API Reference**
  for the allowed transitions and request bodies.
</Note>
